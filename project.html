Project in Electronic Workshop:- Security alarm system

This circuit will help you to guard your precious documents as well as jewellery from intruders or theft. All you need is just to place this circuit in front of the locker or below the mat so when any unknown person come and walk over the switch, the circuit will trigger and sound of alarm comes. The main benefit of the circuit is that these can be implied in two places at a time as two different switches produces two different sounds.

Project in ISA:-Automatic Plant watering system

Automating plant watering can be achieved through various methods, but using Arduino provides a customizable and programmable platform.

The system according to your plant's needs, you can create an effective automatic plant watering system using Arduino. It's a project that combines electronics, programming, and horticulture to help keep your plants healthy and thriving.
